Word Search Puzzle

How to Play
Find all hidden words in the grid! Words can go horizontally, vertically, or diagonally (forwards and backwards).

Swipe or click-drag across letters to select a word. Find all words before time runs out to earn bonus points!

Scoring: +100 per word found, time bonus at completion. Hints cost 50 points.
