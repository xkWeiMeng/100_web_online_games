How to Play

Roll 5 dice up to 3 times per turn. Tap dice to hold them between rolls. After rolling, choose a scoring category on the scorecard. Fill all 13 categories to finish. Score 63+ in the upper section for a 35-point bonus. Roll five of a kind for a Yahtzee worth 50 points!
