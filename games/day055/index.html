How to Play

You play as Red pieces (bottom). Click a piece to select it, then click a highlighted square to move. Pieces move diagonally forward. Jump over enemy pieces to capture them. Reach the opposite end to become a King and move in all diagonal directions. Capture all enemy pieces or block all their moves to win!
