How To Play

  • Pick Rock, Paper, or Scissors to battle the CPU.
  • Win consecutive rounds to build a win streak.
  • Each streak level adds a score multiplier (x2, x3, x4…).
  • Losing or drawing resets your streak.
  • First to 30 points wins the match!
