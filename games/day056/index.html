Reversi

The Classic Strategy Game

How to Play

Place your disc to outflank your opponent's discs. Outflanked discs are flipped to your color.

A valid move must capture at least one opponent disc by trapping it between your discs in any direction (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

The game ends when neither player can move. The player with the most discs wins!
