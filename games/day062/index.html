How to Play

  • A scrambled word appears as letter tiles
  • Click or tap letters to build your answer
  • Click answer letters to send them back
  • You can also type on your keyboard
  • Use hints if you're stuck (costs points)
  • Score bonus points for speed and streaks
  • Solve as many as you can before time runs out!
