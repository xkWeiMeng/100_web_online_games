Nonogram

Picross Logic Puzzle

How to Play

Fill in cells to reveal a hidden picture! The numbers on each row and column tell you how many consecutive filled cells exist in that line.

Left Click — Fill a cell

Right Click / Toggle Mark — Mark cell as empty (X)

Multiple numbers mean multiple groups separated by at least one empty cell.

You have 3 lives — filling an incorrect cell costs a life!
